# Wrestler Landing Page Outline

## Goals
- Present a polished snapshot of the independent wrestler experience on WrestleConnect.
- Showcase credibility signals (recommendations, event history) to help promotions vet talent quickly.
- Encourage wrestlers without profiles to sign up and highlight premium features.

## Page Sections
1. **Hero – Wrestler Persona**  
   - Dynamic headline speaking directly to wrestlers.  
   - Supporting copy about owning your booking narrative.  
   - Primary CTA: “Create Your Profile”. Secondary CTA: “Preview Demo Profile”.  
   - Hero imagery: spotlight photo with gradient overlay, featuring a representative wrestler.

2. **Credibility Proof**  
   - Three-column stats row: Verified recommendations, events logged, average response time.  
   - Carousel of promotion testimonials endorsing top wrestlers.

3. **Profile Showcase**  
   - Split layout: interactive card mock showing media carousel, accolades, availability widget.  
   - Copy explaining how uploads, tags, and embedded matches work.

4. **Event Timeline**  
   - Vertical timeline preview of past events automatically synced from promotion cards.  
   - Callout about how tagging keeps history accurate.

5. **Ratings & Verification**  
   - Pillar section on promotion-issued recommendations with sample rating UI and tag chips.  
   - Copy on dispute flow and visibility control.

6. **Media & Branding Tools**  
   - Grid of features: media gallery, social links, embed support, press kits.  
   - Highlight optional premium analytics.

7. **Community & Support**  
   - Quotes from well-respected trainers or promotions.  
   - Link to knowledge base, safety guidelines, and community standards.

8. **Call to Action Footer**  
   - Re-engage wrestlers with clear benefits list and CTA buttons.  
   - Secondary CTA for promotions to invite their roster.

## Navigation & Routing
- Page route: `/wrestlers`.
- Linked from main navigation alongside Promotions and Schools.
- Consider sticky sub-navigation for in-page anchors on desktop.

## Content Needs
- Sample imagery (licensed or placeholder) representing diverse independent wrestlers.
- Pull data from seed API or mock objects until backend ready.
- Testimonial copy sourced from launch partners (placeholder lorem text until confirmed).
